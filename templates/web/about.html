What we do

Mission : We secure and transform the future of orphan and poor children using technology.

What problem are we solving?

NGO’s and Govt. bodies working for welfare of poor or orphaned children are right now only providing education and support till intermediate level. In most of the cases these children are not able to get higher education or employment due to the lack of professional training and expertise. Resources used on these children goes to waste because they once again return to homelessness, poverty and unemployment.

Orphanages, NGOs and Govt. Institutions are providing basic standard education to these children till intermediate school level. Govt. schemes are provided to support higher education of these children, but only a few of them are able to secure their future.

View More

What is our solution approach

We offer a solution in which orphan and poor children will get education / training of professional software, web development, app development, software engineering, marketing, sales and other professional courses to prepare them according to industry level expertise

Professional courses on software, marketing and entrepreneurship will allow these unprivileged children to have a part-time or freelance job while continuing their higher studies. Entrepreneurship courses can inspire them to innovate and take risks and provide solutions to the world’s largest problems.
